About WaterKit

Water is essential to human health, yet many people forget to drink enough water throughout the day. Chronic dehydration can lead to various health problems.

WaterKit helps you track your hydration in the simplest way, building healthy habits through timely reminders.

Features
  • Customizable hydration reminders
  • Check your hydration status anytime, anywhere
  • Multiple cute character bodies with dynamic water-fill animations
  • Analyze your drinking habits
  • Simple and intuitive statistics and charts
Note

WaterKit integrates with Apple Health to read your weight, biological sex and water records, and write your water and caffeine data.

We use weight and biological sex to calculate your daily recommended hydration goal. We record your water and caffeine intake for your review. You can enable or disable these permissions in Apple Health at any time.

WaterKit is not a medical app. If you intend to use this app for medical purposes or need specific hydration requirements, please consult your doctor.
