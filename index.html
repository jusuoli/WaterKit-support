WaterKit (Drinking Panda) helps you build healthy hydration habits. It brings together daily goals, multi-dimensional charts, iCloud sync across devices, HealthKit integration, Apple Watch complications and an achievement system — all wrapped around a panda that grows with you. Available on iPhone / iPad / Mac / Apple Watch in 24 languages.

03常见问题FAQ

数据能在多台设备间同步吗？ Does my data sync across devices?
可以。WaterKit 使用 iCloud 私有数据库（CloudKit）同步你的饮水记录。请确保设备已登录同一个 Apple ID 并在「设置 → Apple ID → iCloud」中允许 WaterKit 使用 iCloud。
Yes. WaterKit syncs your drink records via iCloud (CloudKit private database). Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID and have allowed WaterKit to use iCloud under Settings → Apple ID → iCloud.
如何把数据导入 Apple「健康」？ How do I send data to Apple Health?
首次授权后，WaterKit 会自动把饮水记录写入健康 App 的「水」类别。你也可以在「设置 → HealthKit」中手动重新授权或从健康 App 反向导入历史数据。
After the first authorization, WaterKit automatically writes drink records to Apple Health under the "Water" category. You can also re-authorize or import historical data from Health in Settings → HealthKit.
订阅怎么取消 / 退款？ How do I cancel a subscription or request a refund?
订阅管理由 Apple 负责：前往「设置 → Apple ID → 订阅」即可取消。退款请通过 reportaproblem.apple.com 申请。WaterKit 提供 3 天免费试用，试用期内取消不收费。
Subscriptions are managed by Apple: cancel at Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. For refunds, use reportaproblem.apple.com. WaterKit offers a 3-day free trial; canceling during the trial incurs no charge.
更换手机后数据还在吗？ Will my data stay after I change my phone?
只要登录同一 Apple ID 并开启 iCloud 同步，在新设备上打开 WaterKit 即会自动恢复记录。卸载重装同理。
As long as you sign in to the same Apple ID with iCloud sync enabled, opening WaterKit on a new device restores your records automatically. Same applies to reinstall.
Widget 或 Watch 不更新怎么办？ What if the Widget or Watch isn't updating?
请确认 App 处于后台运行状态且 iCloud 同步已开启。如仍不刷新，可在 App 内手动添加一条记录触发更新，或重启设备。Watch 端请确认已通过 Watch App 安装且 Complication 已添加到表盘。
Make sure the app is allowed in the background and iCloud sync is on. If it still won't refresh, adding a record in the app forces an update, or try restarting the device. For Watch, confirm it's installed via the Watch app and the complication is added to the face.
