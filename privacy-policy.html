Privacy Policy

最后更新：2026 年 6 月

Last updated: June 2026

WaterKit（以下简称「本应用」）由独立开发者开发运营。我们尊重并保护每一位用户的隐私。本政策说明我们如何处理你的数据。

1. 我们收集的数据

  • 饮水记录：你主动输入的饮水量、饮品类型与时间。这些数据完全由你创建，仅用于应用功能展示与统计。
  • 健康数据（HealthKit）：在你明确授权后，本应用会读写 Apple「健康」中的「水」类别数据，用于同步和去重。健康数据不会离开你的设备与你的 iCloud。
  • iCloud 数据：用于在多设备间同步记录。数据存储于你个人 Apple ID 对应的 CloudKit 私有数据库。
  • 本地偏好：如语言、单位、目标等设置，保存在设备本地 / App Group。

2. 我们不收集的数据

本应用不进行任何用户跟踪，不包含第三方广告 SDK，不收集设备标识符用于广告追踪，也不收集你的位置、通讯录或浏览历史。我们没有任何服务器来存储你的个人数据。

3. 数据如何使用

所有数据仅用于实现应用本身的功能（记录、统计、同步、Widget、Apple Watch），不会出售、共享或用于其他目的。

4. 内购与订阅

订阅与付款由 Apple 处理，本应用不会收到你的支付卡信息。订单与退款请通过 Apple 管理。

5. 数据的删除

你可以随时在应用内删除任意记录或全部数据；卸载应用会清除本地数据；在「系统设置 → Apple ID → iCloud → 管理账户存储」中可清除 iCloud 同步数据。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集儿童个人信息。

7. 联系方式

如有疑问，请邮件至 jusuoli@163.com。

WaterKit (the "App") is developed and operated by an independent developer. We respect and protect every user's privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data We Collect

  • Drink records: Amounts, drink types and timestamps you enter yourself. Created solely by you and used only for app features and statistics.
  • Health data (HealthKit): After your explicit permission, the App reads/writes the "Water" category in Apple Health for sync and de-duplication. Health data never leaves your device and your iCloud.
  • iCloud data: Used to sync records across your devices. Stored in the CloudKit private database of your own Apple ID.
  • Local preferences: Settings such as language, units and goals, kept on-device / App Group.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The App performs no user tracking, contains no third-party ad SDKs, does not collect device identifiers for ad tracking, and does not collect your location, contacts or browsing history. We operate no servers that store your personal data.

3. How Data Is Used

All data is used only to power the app's own features (logging, charts, sync, Widget, Apple Watch). It is never sold, shared or repurposed.

4. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Payments and subscriptions are processed by Apple. The App never receives your card details. Manage orders and refunds through Apple.

5. Deleting Your Data

You can delete any record or all data from within the app at any time. Uninstalling clears local data; iCloud-synced data can be cleared under Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account Storage.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Contact

For questions, email jusuoli@163.com.
